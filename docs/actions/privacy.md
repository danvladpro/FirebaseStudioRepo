# Generate Privacy Policy & Cookie Notice

Generate a combined Privacy Policy and Cookie Notice for my website. Context:

- Product: An Excel shortcut trainer web app
- Business: Sole trader registered in the Netherlands
- International
- GDPR applies (EU users)

The document must cover:

1. **Who we are** — data controller is the sole trader, based in the Netherlands (placeholder for name/address)
2. **What data we collect**
   - Account data (email, name if applicable)
   - Usage data (pages visited, shortcuts practiced — for improving the product)
   - Survey for analytics
   - Payment data 
   - Cookies (see cookie section)
3. **Why we collect it (legal basis under GDPR)**
   - Contract performance (to deliver the service)
   - Legitimate interest (analytics, improving the product)
   - Consent (marketing emails, non-essential cookies)
4. **Who we share it with**
   - Payment processor
   - Analytics provider (Google Analytics)
   - No selling of data to third parties
5. **Retention** — data kept as long as account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
6. **User rights (GDPR)** — right to access, rectify, erase, port, object, and withdraw consent; right to lodge complaint with Dutch DPA (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens)
7. **Cookie notice**
   - Strictly necessary cookies (no consent needed)
   - Analytics cookies (consent required)
   - No advertising/tracking cookies
   - Link to cookie settings / consent banner
8. **Contact** — placeholder for contact email / DPA contact
